Delete this file once you've added your real group photo here.

1. Add your event's group photo to this "images" folder.
   In GitHub: open the images folder > "Add file" > "Upload files".
2. Make sure the file name matches WELCOME_IMAGE in js/config.js
   (it defaults to "images/group-photo.jpg" — either rename your
   photo to match, or edit that line in config.js to match your
   photo's actual file name).
